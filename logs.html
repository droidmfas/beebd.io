Field & Space Mission Logs

Cassini Archival Data • Entry #104
Vortex Core Pressure Profiles

Analysis of thermal infrared imaging shows a high-altitude central warm eye inside the north polar vortex. Temperature differentials of ~15 K indicate persistent downwelling within the central core.

Ground Telescope Survey • Entry #088
Mid-Latitude Storm Outbreaks

Amateur and professional tracking reported sporadic convective disturbances around 42°N latitude. Methane-band filtering reveals high-altitude cloud tops extending past the primary aerosol layer.

Orbital Dynamics • Entry #042
Equatorial Ring Shadowing Effects

As the orbital inclination changes, ring shadow coverage across the northern hemisphere impacts radiative equilibrium, modifying local atmospheric convection rates.
